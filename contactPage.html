Contact Page

Make sure to select the contact page template from your Contact Us page admin.

From the theme editor → About page you can edit:

  • Introduction text (e.g. Our policy is customer first, second, and third...)
  • Form submission success message (e.g. Thanks for contacting us!...)
  • Outro text (e.g. Customer service works M-F 9-5 EST...)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
